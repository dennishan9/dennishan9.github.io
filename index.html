NTNYC
NTNYC is a non-tourist guide to NYC. If you're a person looking for the places that make NYC NYC (and not the safe theme park a tourist would picture in their heads) then this place is for you. We tell you the low down underground information that is not pc but the information that allows you to make an informed decision on how you want to discover the truly gritty and innovative engines that express what this city can be. In so doing we give props to the true innovators - the artists, chefs, musicians, and collectors that don't always get the spotlight, but aren't affraid to stick to their passions and keep this city alive. You can do the tourist thing, but when you're done and ready, you can come here.
